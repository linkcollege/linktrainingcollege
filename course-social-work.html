Social Work & Community Development

Overview

This course prepares students to engage in social work and development at the grassroots and institutional level.

Objectives

  • Understand social work ethics and practice
  • Promote sustainable community development
  • Support vulnerable populations through interventions

Entry Requirements

  • Certificate: KCSE Grade D (Plain)
  • Diploma: KCSE Grade C- (Minus)

Duration

  • Certificate: 1 year
  • Diploma: 2 years
