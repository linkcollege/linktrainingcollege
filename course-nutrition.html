Nutrition and Dietetics

Course Overview

This program equips learners with nutritional skills to assess, plan, and deliver dietary interventions and promote wellness.

Objectives

  • Assess and analyze nutritional needs
  • Plan appropriate diets and interventions
  • Support food safety, hygiene, and public health

Entry Requirements

  • C- (Minus) in KCSE with at least D+ in Biology/Chemistry for Diploma
  • D (Plain) in KCSE with at least D in Biology/Chemistry for Certificate

Duration

  • Diploma: 6 semesters
  • Certificate: 4 semesters
