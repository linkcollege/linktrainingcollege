Procurement

Overview

This program equips students with supply chain, purchasing, and logistics skills for competitive markets.

Objectives

  • Understand procurement regulations and ethics
  • Develop inventory and vendor management skills
  • Prepare for both private and public sector roles

Entry Requirements

  • Certificate: KCSE D Plain
  • Diploma: KCSE C- (Minus)

Duration

  • Certificate: 1 year
  • Diploma: 2 years
