Leadership & Theology

Overview

This course offers foundational training in Biblical leadership, discipleship, and church development.

Objectives

  • Equip church leaders with theological grounding
  • Foster servant leadership and mentorship
  • Prepare students for ministry and mission

Entry Requirements

  • Certificate: KCSE D (Plain)
  • Diploma: KCSE C- (Minus)

Duration

  • Certificate: 1 year
  • Diploma: 2 years
