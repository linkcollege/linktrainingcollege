About LINK Training College

LINK Training College is a Christ-centered institution based in Marsabit, Kenya, committed to excellence, professionalism, and service to the community through transformative education. We exist to equip students spiritually, academically, and practically to serve and impact society in meaningful ways.

We offer diverse certificate and diploma programs in fields like Community Health, Nutrition, Social Work, Information Technology, Human Resource Management, Theology, and more. Our teaching is rooted in practical experience, mentorship, and strong values.

Whether you're seeking to begin your career or expand your professional skills, LINK is your training ground for personal transformation and community service.
