Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a course?

Visit our Apply page and fill in the online application form. Once submitted, our admissions office will contact you.

What courses are offered at LINK Training College?

We offer programs in Community Health, Nutrition and Dietetics, Social Work, ICT, Theology, HRM, and more. Visit the Courses page for full details.

Where is the college located?

We are located in Marsabit, Kenya. For more details, visit the Contact page.

Do you offer certificates and diplomas?

Yes! We offer both certificate and diploma programs, registered and approved by TVET/CDACC.

Is accommodation provided?

We currently assist students in finding safe and affordable accommodation nearby.
