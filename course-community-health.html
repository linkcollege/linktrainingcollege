Community Health

Course Overview

This course equips students with essential skills in preventive and primary health care, health education, sanitation, and community outreach programs.

Course Objectives

  • Understand principles of community-based health care
  • Train students in hygiene, immunization, and nutrition
  • Prepare graduates for community health outreach roles

Entry Requirements

  • Minimum KCSE Grade D (Plain) for Certificate level
  • Minimum KCSE Grade C- (Minus) for Diploma level

Duration

  • Certificate: 1 year
  • Diploma: 2 years
